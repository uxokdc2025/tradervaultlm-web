Quick Answers

Common questions answered

Find quick answers about features, pricing, brokers, prop firms, copy trading, and how the platform works before you begin.

What is TraderVaultLM.ai?

TraderVaultLM.ai is a connected trading platform that helps traders create or upload strategies, run backtests, analyze performance, and manage live trading workflows from one system.

Do I need my own strategy to use the platform?

No. You can start by building a strategy inside the platform or uploading existing signal logic to begin testing and analysis.

How does AI fit into the platform?

AI is used to assist with analysis by helping traders compare winning and losing trades, identify patterns, and surface insights that support better strategy decisions.

How is TraderVaultLM.ai different from using separate tools?

Most traders rely on separate tools for charting, backtesting, analysis, brokers, and copy trading. TraderVaultLM.ai brings those workflows together into one connected system to reduce friction and improve visibility.

Can I try TraderVaultLM.ai before subscribing?

Yes. You can explore the platform with a 3-day free trial to see how it fits your trading workflow before choosing a plan.

Does TraderVaultLM.ai support backtesting?

Yes. The platform is designed to help traders backtest strategies against historical conditions so they can better understand performance before moving into live execution.

Does the platform support live trading?

Yes. The platform is designed to support copy-trading workflows, including master and follower account structures for more organized multi-account execution.
